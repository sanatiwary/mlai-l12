natural language processing
- branch of ML and AI that deals with computer understanding human language (converting to binary)
- applications: smart device, smart assistant (siri, alexa, google assistant), chat bots
- sentence in any language -> mathematical/numerical representation

1. identify and remove stopwords
2. stemming/lemmatization: convert all words to base (remove past tense, ing)
3. vectorization: converting words to binary format

sentiment analysis: identifying positive/negative language to classify "sentiment"
- ex. product reviews, facebook comments, etc.
- language like "good", "love/like" -> positive; language like "bad", "don't like" -> negative
